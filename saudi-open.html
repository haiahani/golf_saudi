TOURNAMENT OVERVIEW

The Saudi Open is part of a group of events that Golf Saudi holds annually to develop raise awareness of the game in the Kingdom as well as developing home-grown future talent. By holding such events, Golf Saudi is looking to increase the number of golfers throughout the Kingdom within all age groups and demographics. By working with other sports federations and the Ministry of Sport, Golf Saudi is looking to underline the wider benefit of sports participation more generally as part of Saudi Arabia’s progress towards its Vision 2030 goals to improve its citizens quality of life.

All professional and Arab amateurs can join and the player who gets the highest points qualifies to play and participate in the Saudi International Tournament.

  • The Saudi Open is the headline event in the Kingdom outside of the international tours.
  • The event crowns the top professional and amateur within the Kingdom.

RIYADH GOLF CLUB

Riyadh Golf Club is a Golf Saudi managed golf facility, employing the very best individuals, providing operational excellence and a first-class experience to all golf and non-golf visitors.
Combined with grass roots training and educational programmes that introduce people to the game at the earliest opportunity, Golf Saudi is committed to delivering a dynamic national development programme that transforms the golfing landscape.
Riyadh Golf Club is located just 20 minutes from Central Riyadh, showcasing a whole host of fantastic golf and leisure facilities.
Riyadh Golf Club is the go-to recreational and social getaway from the capital. The Club provides fun and exciting golf experiences for beginners to established players whether men, women or children.
With an attractive range of facilities for families, members and corporate communities, Riyadh Golf Club offers memorable visits to all.
