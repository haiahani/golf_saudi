Dirab golf & country club

Dirab Golf & Country Club offers a challenging championship golf course, measuring an impressive 7280 yards from the black tees. The course, which is situated within the Tawfiq Valley, offers a variety of undulating fairways, elevated tee boxes and tree-lined fairways to truly test golfers of all abilities.OO
The course’s spectacular backdrop and practice facilities make Dirab Golf & Country Club one of the most desirable facilities in Saudi Arabia, showcasing some of the best talent in both amateur and professional golf tournaments. With 4 interesting par 5s, the course provides a number of birdie opportunities, however each individual hole is carefully designed with a variety of hazards, tree-lined tee shots, and challenging greens with subtle breaks

RIYADH GOLF CLUB

Riyadh Golf Club is a Golf Saudi managed golf facility, employing the very best individuals, providing operational excellence and a first-class experience to all golf and non-golf visitors. Combined with grass roots training and educational programmes that introduce people to the game at the earliest opportunity, Golf Saudi is committed to delivering a dynamic national development programme that transforms the golfing landscape.
Riyadh Golf Club is located just 20 minutes from Central Riyadh, showcasing a whole host of fantastic golf and leisure facilities. Riyadh Golf Club is the go-to recreational and social getaway from the capital. The Club provides fun and exciting golf experiences for beginners to established players whether men, women or children. With an attractive range of facilities for families, members and corporate communities, Riyadh Golf Club offers memorable visits to all.
