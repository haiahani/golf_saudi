Golf Saudi Federation

The Saudi Golf Federation (SGF) is the body responsible for governing the game of golf in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia. With ambitious development programmes already put in place for golf to gather momentum in the Kingdom, the SGF is committed to providing the infrastructure and governance required for the game to flourish. New golfers will take up the game and a new national sport will be born, inspiring a new generation to compete. It’s an exciting time for golf in Saudi Arabia.

NAFES

NAFES is a unique platform launched by the Ministry of Sport, as part of the ‘Quality of Life’ program and under the umbrella of Vision 2030. Designed to welcome the private sector into the Saudi Sport, ‘NAFES’ offers a world class platform to issue licenses for private sport clubs, sport academies and sport centers. The platform aims to elevate the performance level of the athletes, grow the sport economy, and increase the contribution of sport to the GDP.

Learn the game

Now is the time to play golf - especially with the number of budding programs being launched at golf clubs across the Kingdom. There is no better way to socialize or experience competition than to practice at a golf club on and off the course.
Golf is more than just a sport. It provides opportunities to learn important interpersonal skills, offers great physical and mental health benefits, and can provide an opportunity to compete nationally.

Events

Golf is not only distinguished by being a game played by millions around the world, but it is also considered a hub for the establishment of a number of the most exciting and exciting international entertainment events.
During the year 2020, the Kingdom hosted a group of the most prominent stars of the game at the Saudi International Golf Championship, which was held at the “Royal Greens” golf course and club in King Abdullah Economic City, with the support of SoftBank Investment Consulting as part of the European Tour.

Golf Nowadays

Golf is changing as it breaks down barriers that build on new technologies, it attracts a younger audience and positions itself as a recreational activity for the whole family.

New forms of the game are also being introduced around the world, and Golf Saudi will be at the forefront of this by opening many golf courses across the Kingdom. This will include indoor simulators, e-golf spots, adventure golf and late-night entertainment facilities.

Golf Equipment

Traditional golf is played with a mix of game-specific equipment, including putters and balls, that are manufactured by many different suppliers and distributed through golf course stores and online retailers.
With the support of beginner and group programs at golf courses in Riyadh and Jeddah, Golf Saudi is expected to produce a "How to Play" booklet in the coming weeks to help all beginners learn the basics of the game.
